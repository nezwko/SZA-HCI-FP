What Are Birds?

Birds are fascinating animals that come in many shapes, sizes, and colors. They share a few special traits that make them unique:

1. Birds Have Feathers

Feathers help birds fly, stay warm, and look beautiful. They come in many colors and patterns.

2. Birds Have Beaks

Birds use their beaks to eat, build nests, and protect themselves. Some beaks are sharp for hunting, while others are flat for eating seeds.

3. Birds Can Fly

Most birds can fly using their wings. Some, like penguins, can’t fly but are great swimmers or runners.

4. Birds Lay Eggs

Most birds lay eggs, and baby birds hatch from these eggs. Parents care for them until they’re ready to fly.

5. Birds Are Warm-Blooded

Birds can keep their bodies warm, no matter the weather. This helps them live in different environments.

6. Birds Come in All Sizes

Birds range from tiny hummingbirds to giant ostriches. Some fly, others run or swim.
