Instructions: Guess the Animal Game! 🐾

Welcome to the Guess the Animal Game! In this fun challenge, you’ll see pictures of animals and type your answers to guess what they are. Are you ready to test your animal knowledge? Let’s get started!

1. Look at the Picture:

A picture of an animal will appear on your screen. Study it carefully! Notice details like its shape, size, color, and special features—does it have fur, feathers, scales, or maybe spots? 🖼️

2. Type Your Answer:

Use your keyboard to type the name of the animal or its type (e.g., "elephant" or "tiger"). Think about what you’ve learned about animals and use your best guess! 🐘

3. Submit Your Answer:

When you’re confident with your guess, press the Submit button to check if you’re correct! 🏆
